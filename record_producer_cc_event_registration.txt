================================================================================
 EVENT REGISTRATION
================================================================================

 Navigate to: System Policy > Events > Registry > New

 Configuration:
 ----------------
 Suffix:        record_producer.submitted
 Table:         <Your Record Producer target table>  (e.g. sc_req_item, or your custom table)
 Description:   Fired when a record producer submission is created.
                Used to trigger the notification that includes CC recipients.
 Application:   <Your scoped app or Global>

================================================================================
 NOTES
================================================================================
 - The event name will be: <table_name>.record_producer.submitted
   For example, if your target table is sc_req_item, the full event name
   would be: sc_req_item.record_producer.submitted
 - This event is fired by the Business Rule (see companion file).
 - The Notification listens for this event to send the email.
